Tsunami Mamis Sticker Design

This sticker design was created for the Tsunami Mamis as a reward for being the most involved group in my university's Nikkei Student Union club. It integrates a traditional Japanese art style with contemporary design techniques, illustrating a powerful and beautiful scene of a koi fish jumping out of the ocean.
