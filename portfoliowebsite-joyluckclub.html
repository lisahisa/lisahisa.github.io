The Joy Luck Club Book Jacket Redesign

Amy Tan's The Joy Luck Club is a classic novel about four Chinese American immigrant families who use their game of mahjong to tell each other stories, discussing themes of family, immigration, sacrifice, preservation of heritage, and cultural identity. The redesigned book cover and inner guts give a renewed, contemporary look to the story, with visual elements that portray the beauty of Asian culture and the powerful nature of storytelling.
